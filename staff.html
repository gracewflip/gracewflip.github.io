Career

If you are a California licensed acupuncturist and passionate about taking care of patients, we are the right clinic for you. We are looking for people who are driven by the pursuit of excellence and committed to making a difference in healthcare.



Job Title:

Acupuncturist



Job Duties:

Develop, implement & coordinate acupuncture therapy and treatment plans with patients. Conduct a detailed diagnosis and collect data on patients' life style, diet, health history, and symptoms before developing such treatment plan; Diagnose health problems by reviewing patients' health and medical histories. Provide recognized and approved techniques using extensive knowledge about the different anatomical points of the human body as they relate to various forms of acupuncture, including the 365 acupuncture points in the human body. Identify correct anatomical and proportional point locations based on patient's figure scale, pain location and body positions to determine the size and count of the needles to insert. Comply with all policies, procedures, and protocols of the clinic.



Degree Required:

Master’s degree in Oriental Medicine and current acupuncture licensure in the State of California and adheres to all standards of professional conduct in keeping license current, including continuing education hours and maintenance of certificates of completion.
